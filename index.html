About Paws & Claws

Paws & Claws is dedicated to connecting loving families with their perfect pet companions. Our team of animal lovers is committed to providing the best care and support for all our furry, feathered, and scaly friends.

With years of experience in animal welfare, we strive to create a welcoming environment for both pets and their potential owners. Visit us today to find your new best friend!
